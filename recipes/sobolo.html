Sobolo (Hibiscus Iced Tea)

Description

Sobolo is a refreshing Ghanaian drink made from dried hibiscus petals (also known as "roselle"). Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, it's enjoyed chilled and often infused with ginger, pineapple, or cloves for a zesty taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried hibiscus petals
  • 1 medium pineapple (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon cloves
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of fresh ginger (grated)
  • 5 cups water
  • Sugar or honey (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Wash the hibiscus petals thoroughly.
  2. In a large pot, combine petals, pineapple chunks, cloves, and ginger.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 15â€“20 minutes.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool, then strain through a fine sieve.
  5. Add sugar or honey to taste and stir well.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator. Serve cold over ice.
